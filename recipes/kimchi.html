Kimchi Recipe

Simple and authentic napa cabbage kimchi recipe

This is a classic and easy Korean food recipe. Napa cabbage Kimchi can be served as a side dish to accompany any meal of your choice.I love it with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium napa cabbage

  • 1/4 cup kosher salt or iodine-free salt

  • water

  • 1 tbsp grated garlic

  • 1tsp grated ginger

  • 1tsp granulated sugar

  • 2 tbsp fish sauce 3 tbsp water

  • 1-5 tbsp Korean red pepper flakes (gochugaru)

  • 25g Korean radish or daikon radish, peeled and cut into matchsticks

  • 4 medium scallions, trimmed and cut into 1 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Cut the cabbage:Cut the cabbage lengthwise through the stem into quaters and cut the cores from each piece. Cut each quarter crosswise into 2 inch-wide strips.

  2. Salt the cabbage:Put the cabbage in a large bowland sprinkle with salt. Masssage with your hands until it is softened a little bit. Add water to cover the cabbage. Put a plate or something on top to weigh it down and let it stand for 1-2 hours.

  3. Rinse and drain the cabbage:Rinse the cabbage under cold water 3 times and set aside in a colander for 15-20 minutes.

  4. Make the paste:Add garlic, ginger,sugar and fish sauce or water in aclean bowl and stir into a smooth paste. Stir in the gocharagu and set aside.

  5. Combine the vegetables and spice paste:Squeeze any remaining water from the cabbage and add it into the spice paste, then add the raddish and scallions.

  6. Mix thoroughly:Gently use your hands to mix the paste into the vegetables until thoroughly coated.

  7. Put the kimchi into a jar:Put the kimchi into ajar and press down on it until the brine rises to cover the vegetables, leaving at least 1 inch of space on top. Seal the jar.

  8. Let it ferment for 1-5 days:Place a bowl or plate under the jar to help catch any overflow and let it stand at cool room temprature out of direct sunlight.

  9. Check it daily and refrigerate when ready:Check it daily and press down with a spoon to keep them submerged in the brine and to release gases.Taste and when it is good enough for your liking, refrigerate it.

    10. You can eat it right away, but it is best after another week or two. Enjoy!
