Chapati recipe

Soft layered chapati recipe

Chapati is an un-leavened flatbread usually made from all-purpose wheat flour, water and oil.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour

  • 3 tbsp oil

  • 1 tsp salt

  • 1 ¼ cups water

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine flour, salt and oil then mix thoroughly. Add water alittle at a time to form a soft and sticky dough.

  2. Put in a floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic. Place ina bowl, cover and let it rest for 20-30 minutes.

  3. Divide the dough into 8 equal parts and roll each into a circle.

  4. Lightly brush the rolled out chapati with some oil and sprinkle some flour then roll it to form a rope-like shape and coil then pull the tip towards the center of the coil and tuck in using your index finger. Cover with a damp towel and leave to rest for about 10-15 minutes.

  5. Flour your work surface and roll out each of the coiled dough to your desired thickness.

  6. Preheat a non-stick pan. Place the chapati and let it cook for a few seconds then turn and do the same to the other side.

  7. Brush a little oil on the chapati and flip then let it cook. Flip and brush oil to the other side and let it cook to your desired brownness.

    8. Serve hot or warm with your favorite stew or tea and enjoy!
